About Me

Hello, my name is Shane Fleming.  I am an aspiring computer scientist at Texas State University.  I was born and raised in the great state of Texas.  I have been coding since my junior year of high school, where my shop teacher introduced me to python.  Since, I have worked and have taken classes in C++, Java, Python, HTML, and CSS.  Outside of the classroom I have worked at the Texas School Safety Center for the last three years as a System Administrator.

Beyond the screen, I am an avid soccer fan and player.  I also enjoy rally cross racing in my WRX.  When I am not behind the wheel, and I enjoy recording and editing film for the other local racers.
