About Me

Hello, my name is Shane Fleming. I am currently a System Analyst at the Texas School Safety Center at Texas State University. My passion in life centers around cultivating relationships and the opportunity to analyze and fix complex problems. I thrive in collaborative environments by enhancing my own personal knowledge and achieving success as a team. In my own time, I enjoy building and creating new projects with Raspberry Pi(s), such as my Gate Timer I built to record lap times while racing motocross.

I have been coding since high school, when my shop teacher introduced me to Python. Since, I have taken classes in and worked extensively with coding languages such as C++, Python, HTML, and CSS. I started in November of 2021 the Software Developer Bootcamp at Austin Community College and I am on track to graduate in July of 2022. I hope to refine my knowledge of front end and back end technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, React, Ruby, NodeJS, ExpressJS, Building APIs, MongoDB, SQL) including the design, development, and maintenance.
