About Me and My Work

I am currently a junior at Texas State University, studying computer science. I have a wide range of technical, and theory based experience. I am currently employed by Texas State University as a tech Support - Student Worker at the Texas School Safety Center and seeking an internship that would challenge me, and expand my knowledge in computer science.

My interest in computers started later then most. My parents had dial up connection, until I graduated high school. My thirst for knowledge came from an auto technology teacher my junior year of high school. Mr. Mitchell pushed me to always ask why, especially when a subject fascinated me. This was very dominant once the electrical part of the course started. I wanted to learn more, and more about the operations of integrated circuits. Which lead to my interest in logic gates, and finally computer science. What I learned while Mr. Mitchell still blessed this earth, still drives me today, in classes such as Digital Logic. The thirst that he bestowed upon me will never leave me, because of this I will continuously strive to gain more knowledge.
